Pfister Comet SR

Price: $1.145.000

Car Class: Sports

Laptime: 1:01.962

The Pfister Comet SR is a sports car featured in Grand Theft Auto Online as part of the continuation of The Doomsday Heist update. The Comet SR is a racing-oriented variant of the Comet, sporting a roll cage and updated rear spoiler by default. It features an updated front and rear fascia, as well as several CFRP elements throughout the body. The vehicle is based on the Porsche 997 GT2 RS, as evidenced in the carbon fiber elements, default roll cage, and default rear spoiler.
